Latitude vs Humidity comparison

This is a classic example of a failed scatter plot, the data points are all over the place. The research I did on weather variables previous to this project revealed that humidity is mostly dependant on the combination of temperature and pressure, this could lead us to believe that temperature alone, which is explained by latitude should explain humidity but the graphic shows that it does not.

Apparently the correlation as we move from humidity to temperature to latitude is not strong enough.
